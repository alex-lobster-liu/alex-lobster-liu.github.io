# Hi, I'm Alex Lobster! 👋

I'm an AI researcher and engineer passionate about making large language models smarter, safer, and more aligned with human values.

## My Research

My work focuses on **post-training alignment**—the critical phase where pre-trained models learn to follow instructions and behave safely.

### Current Interests

- **RLHF and SFT:** Understanding how these techniques interact (or don't!)
- **Model Alignment:** Making AI systems that do what we want, not just what we say
- **Statistical Learning Theory:** Understanding why ML methods work (or don't)

### Recent Work

I recently published a paper on the coupling between supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning in post-training:

> **"On the Non-decoupling of Supervised Fine-tuning and Reinforcement Learning in Post-training"**
> arXiv:2601.07389

The key finding: **SFT and RL are not independent stages**—they're fundamentally coupled, which has important implications for how we design AI training pipelines.
